WheatNet v1.0 Release Notes

WheatNet v1.0 contains 420,002 concepts and 1,609,688 relations. The bread wheat genome in WheatNet is version 1.0 of the chromosome survey sequence for Triticum aestivum cv. Chinese Spring generated by the International Wheat Genome Sequencing Consortium. The gene models are provided by MIPS (version 2.0).
