Drosophilla melanogaster Release Notes
 

The Drosophilla melanogaster (BDGP6) information network contains:

    Datasets used for integration

    • Gene (Version: 5b; Coding genes: 14,156)
      • Drosophila_melanogaster.BDGP6.28.gff3 from Ensembl
    • Protein (Version: 5b; Proteins: 30,362)
      • Drosophila_melanogaster.BDGP6.pep.all.fa from Ensembl
    • Protein-Domain
      • BioMart InterPro Protein Domains
      • BioMart PFam Protein Domains
    • References
      • Drosophila melanogaster (FlyBase), see detail reports/information for Drosophila melanogaster
      • Drosphila melanogaster(UniProt) - Information about well reviewed Drosphila melanogaster proteins
        UniProt: taxonomy:7227 AND reviewed:yes
    • Homology
      • BioMart Drosophila homologs in Human, Mouse and Yeast
      • Decypher Tera-Blast of Drosphila melanogaster against 11 species under Drosphila genus
    • Ontologies & Mappings
      • gene_ontology.1_2.obo
      • interpro2go
      • pfam2go
